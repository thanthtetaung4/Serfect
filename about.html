Vision and Mission

Vision 1

To become the premier online destination for travelers visiting Singapore by providing high-quality, personalized travel information, and resources that enable them to experience the best of Singapore, while promoting sustainable tourism and environmental stewardship

Vision 2

To make travel to Singapore accessible and affordable to all by providing a user-friendly online platform that offers competitive pricing, a wide range of travel options, and exceptional customer service.

Missions

  1. To provide comprehensive and accurate information about hotels, tours, and travel services in Singapore that help visitors make informed decisions about their travel plans.
  2. To promote sustainable tourism by highlighting environmentally responsible practices and encouraging travelers to support local communities and businesses.
  3. To offer personalized travel recommendations and resources that help visitors create memorable and authentic experiences in Singapore.
  4. To provide a user-friendly platform that makes it easy for travelers to search for and book their travel plans in Singapore.
  5. To offer exceptional customer service that provides travelers with the support and assistance they need before, during, and after their trip to Singapore.
