Luxury Package

  • Costs:Staring from SGD 8000
  • Days: 5 days,4 nights

   Welcome to the height of luxury in Singapore! From the moment you arrive via private aircraft, you will be treated like royalty.

Day 1

   Upon your arrival in Singapore via private aircraft, a chauffeur will greet you and transport you to the iconic Marina Bay Sands Hotel. Take in the breathtaking view of the city from your opulent penthouse room, complete with floor-to-ceiling windows. Combat jet lag with a private spa treatment at the Banyan Tree Spa, located within the hotel. In the evening, indulge in a two-Michelin-starred meal at Odette, known for its modern French cuisine and impressive wine list.

Day 2

   Start your day with a private yacht cruise around Sentosa Island, taking in the sea breeze and luxurious amenities on board. Take a swim at one of the isolated beaches before returning to the yacht for lunch. In the afternoon, visit the Supertree Grove in Gardens by the Bay, or play a round of golf at Sentosa Golf Club, one of Asia's best golf clubs. In the evening, take a private helicopter tour of the city, flying over some of Singapore's most well-known sites, before enjoying an outstanding four-Michelin-starred meal at Les Amis.

Day 3

   Learn more about Singapore's diverse culture with a private city tour. Visit Chinatown's traditional shophouses, Bugis' avant-garde art galleries, or Holland Village's vintage stores. Take in the award-winning orchid displays and have a picnic at the UNESCO-listed Botanic Gardens. In the afternoon, indulge in some retail therapy at upscale shopping districts like ION Orchard before enjoying Michelin-starred Chinese cuisine at Imperial Treasure Fine Chinese Cuisine.

Day 4

   Enjoy a decadent breakfast at Jaan, located in a sensuous penthouse in the city's core, before indulging in a private personal shopping service at upscale shopping centers like Marina Bay Sands. Continue on to a private horseback riding session and trail at the Singapore Polo Club before rounding off your evening with a gourmet dinner served aboard a private yacht and a helicopter tour of the local islands.

Day 5

   On your final day in Singapore, enjoy a superb room service supper in your penthouse suite while admiring the expansive view of the city. This will be a wonderful way to say goodbye before you depart from Singapore.

   Thank you for considering our luxury tour package of Singapore. We are confident that this five-day experience will leave you with memories to last a lifetime. With opulent accommodations at the world-renowned Marina Bay Sands Hotel, breathtaking views of the city, and a range of exclusive activities, including a private yacht cruise, helicopter tour, spa treatments, and fine dining at Michelin-starred restaurants, this is the ultimate indulgence.
