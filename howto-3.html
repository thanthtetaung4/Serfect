Car rental services


There are various car rental companies in Singapore, such as Avis, Budget, and Hertz, that offer short-term and long-term rental options.


  • Avis Car Rental - located at Changi Airport and in the city center at Orchard Road.

  • Budget Car Rental - has several locations across the island including Changi Airport, Downtown, Jurong East, and Woodlands.

  • Hertz Car Rental - has several locations across the island including Changi Airport, Downtown, Jurong East, and Woodlands.

  • Europcar - has several locations across the island including Changi Airport and Orchard Road.

  • Sixt Car Rental - located at Changi Airport and in the city center at Chinatown.

  • Thrifty Car Rental - has several locations across the island including Changi Airport and Orchard Road.

  • Car Club - offers car-sharing services and has several locations across the island including Changi Airport and Bukit Batok.

Bicycle rental services


Singapore has an extensive network of cycling paths, and there are several bicycle rental services, such as Bike Share, that offer affordable rates.


  • Bike Share - has multiple bike rental stations across the island including in Sentosa, Gardens by the Bay, and East Coast Park.

  • SG Bike - offers bike-sharing services with rental stations located across the island including in Tampines, Jurong Lake Gardens, and Bukit Panjang.

  • Anywheel - offers bike-sharing services with rental stations located in the city center, including in Clarke Quay, Bugis, and Chinatown.


Equipment rental services


From cameras and video cameras to party equipment and power tools, there are many rental services available for equipment in Singapore.


  • Rent Tycoons - offers rental services for power tools, party equipment, medical equipment, and more. Located in Bukit Merah.

  • A-Plant Rental - provides rental services for construction and industrial equipment, located in Kranji.

  • Camera Rental Centre - offers rental services for cameras and video equipment, located in Funan Mall.

  • Jtech Automation & Engineering - provides rental services for audio-visual equipment, event lighting, and more. Located in Ang Mo Kio.
