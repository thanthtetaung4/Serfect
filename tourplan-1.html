Budget Package

  • Costs:SGD 600
  • Days: 5 days,4 nights

   Welcome to Singapore! Get ready for an exciting adventure filled with delicious food, stunning scenery, and unforgettable experiences.

Day 1

   We'll start by visiting the iconic Jewel Changi Airport, where you'll be greeted by the world's tallest indoor waterfall and beautiful greenery. You'll also have the chance to explore the Canopy Park, which offers fun attractions like a hedge maze and sky nets. After a day of exploring, check into CapsulePods, where you can rest in a private sleeping pod with amenities like Wi-Fi and common restrooms. For lunch, we'll head to the renowned Singapore ZamZam Indian restaurant, known for their mouth-watering briyani and murtabak dishes. Take a stroll through Kampong Glam and Haji Lane to soak in the colorful street art and unique shopping options. No trip to Singapore is complete without visiting the Merlion statue and Garden by the Bay!

Day 2

   We will start with a delicious breakfast at a Hawker Centre, followed by a visit to the Singapore Zoo where you'll get to see an amazing animal show. Explore Chinatown's vibrant atmosphere and visit the impressive Buddha Tooth Relic Temple and Museum. End the day at Clarke Quay, where you can enjoy a scrumptious dinner and drinks while taking in the beautiful views of the Singapore River.

Day 3

   We'll start with a flavorful Singaporean breakfast of laksa before heading to Sentosa Island. Explore the mesmerizing marine life at the Sea Aquarium and soak in the stunning views at Siloso Beach. After dinner at one of Sentosa's many restaurants, return to your hotel feeling content and fulfilled after a day of adventure.

Day 4

   This day is all about shopping, dining, and sightseeing! Enjoy a satisfying breakfast at a local hawker center before exploring the outlet mall in Jurong East. Indulge in a hot pot lunch at Shi Jian Hot Pot before taking a tour of the city on the Big Bus from Suntec City Mall. End the day with a delicious dinner at Suntec City, featuring a variety of local and international cuisines. Catch the stunning Garden Rhapsody light and sound show in the evening, and for dinner head to Marina Square for a range of dining options. Finish the day with the spectacular Singapore Water Show, a must-see choreographed performance of water jets and lights.

Day 5

   After having breakfast,we will be led to the Changi airport for departure.

   We hope you enjoy your Singapore adventure and create unforgettable memories along the way!
