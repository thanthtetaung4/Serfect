Premium Package

  • Costs:SGD 3000
  • Days: 5 days,4 nights

   Welcome to Serfect, your ultimate guide to the best travel experiences. If you're planning a trip to Singapore and want to make the most of your time there, our Best Offer 3000 Package is just for you. With this 5-day, 4-night package, you can explore the city's top attractions, savor the best cuisine, and have a comfortable stay at the Siloso Beach Resort Sentosa.

Day 1: Arrival and Sentosa Island

   Your trip begins with a meet and greet at the airport with your private transfer. We'll take you to the Siloso Beach Resort Sentosa, where you can check in and freshen up. Next, we'll take you to the Jewel Changi Airport, where you can enjoy the stunning indoor waterfall and various attractions. Later, head to the S.E.A Aquarium and enjoy the SkyHelix Sentosa, an exciting 360-degree ride. Have lunch at the Blue Lotus Restaurant Sentosa Cove, followed by some leisure time at Siloso Beach. End your day with dinner at the Royal Taj in Sentosa for an unforgettable Indian food experience.

Day 2: Downtown Singapore

   After breakfast at the resort, we'll take you to the iconic Merlion statue in the heart of Singapore's Central Business District (CBD). Explore the towering skyscrapers and relax at the Singapore Botanic Garden. Enjoy a delicious sushi lunch at Chojiro, followed by a visit to the Urban Park at Guoco Tower. Take a trip to the Singapore City Gallery to see a scale model of the city, followed by VR gaming at Holgate Singapore. In the evening, bike around Marina Bay and take a four-course private sky dining experience in the Singapore Flyer while admiring the magnificent view of Singapore's skyline.

Day 3: Universal Studios and Sentosa Island

   After breakfast at the resort, spend the whole day at Universal Studios and enjoy a sumptuous Italian lunch at Curate Cucina Pisana. Later, take a break at the resort before heading to the Wings of Time show at 8 pm, a spectacular display of lights, water, and fire. Have dinner at the Candlenut restaurant to savor the best local Singaporean cuisine.

Day 4: Orchard Road and Flower Dome

   After breakfast at the resort, head to the famous Orchard Road for some shopping and indulge in the most recommended ice-cream sandwich. Shop around the Orchard Road and enjoy European cuisine at Winestone (Orchard). Visit the Flower Dome in the afternoon and experience the beauty of nature. In the evening, head to Clarke Quay and witness the bustling nightlife of Singapore. End your day with a city lights dinner at the Royal Albatross Cruise.

Day 5: Departure

   Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at the resort and take some time to relax before your departure. We'll transfer you back to the airport, where you can catch your flight back home
