Recommended Package

  • Costs:SGD 1000
  • Days: 5 days,4 nights

   Welcome to Singapore! Get ready for an exciting adventure filled with delicious food, stunning scenery, and unforgettable experiences.

Day 1

   You'll start your trip with a visit to Jewel Changi Airport, a stunning mixed-use development that houses numerous shops, restaurants, and attractions. Afterward, you'll check into Haising Hotel before heading to Singapore ZamZam Indian restaurant for lunch. In the afternoon, you'll explore the colorful Haji Lane and Kampong Glam areas, known for their quirky finds and street art. In the evening, you'll visit the iconic Merlion statue and watch a performance at the Esplanade Theatre. Finally, you'll enjoy dinner and drinks at a restaurant in the Esplanade Theatre and watch the Singapore Water Show before returning to your hotel.

Day 2

   You'll start yo ur day with a delicious breakfast at a local food court before heading to the Singapore Zoo. Here, you'll explore the rainforest environment and watch an animal show. Afterward, you'll have lunch at a nearby restaurant before taking a leisurely stroll through the historic Chinatown area and visiting the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple and Museum. In the evening, you'll head to Clarke Quay for dinner and drinks before returning to your hotel.

Day 3

   You'll begin your day with a breakfast of Singapore's signature dish, laksa, before heading to Sentosa Island. Here, you'll spend the entire day at Universal Studios, enjoying thrilling rides and attractions. After having lunch at Universal Studios, you'll stay until 8pm to watch a night show before having dinner at a food court in Vivocity @Harbourfront. Finally, you'll return to your hotel for the night.

Day 4

   You'll start your day by trying rice porridge at a local hawker center before visiting Gardens by the Bay. Here, you'll enter the Cloud Forest and Flower Dome and marvel at the stunning flora and fauna. Afterward, you'll have lunch at the Skypark Observation Deck before heading to Orchard Road for shopping. Finally, you'll have dinner at one of the shopping malls in Orchard Road before returning to your hotel.

Day 5

   You'll have breakfast at your hotel before heading back to the airport to conclude your trip. We hope this breakdown helps you better understand your itinerary and what to expect during your trip to Singapore!

   Thank you for considering these packages to enhance your travel experience. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or delicious cuisine, there's a package to suit your needs. With these options, your trip is sure to be full of joy and unforgettable moments. Safe travels, and we hope you have a wonderful journey!
